The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

We want our country to be recognized with respect as a democratic nation in the world. The plans for the country should be centered around the people, not centered around any leader or party. We have witnessed the implementation of projects by the autocratic Hasina government that have squandered millions of unnecessary taxpayer money, which were never for the benefit of the people, but rather involved ugly self-interest and massive theft. We have already begun the process of scrutinizing all ongoing and proposed development projects. Considering the stage of project implementation, we will look for ways to reduce costs in the remaining works and will even consider canceling them if necessary. The people of the country will no longer be deceived. We will prioritize projects that create employment.
Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
